My top 3 space movies

What would you do if the return operation from you mission to the red plant unexpectedly collapse at the last moment and you have to survive by your self just counting on your ingenuity?. A great movie directed by Ridley Scott.

Interstellar

Our planet faces it doom and we must migrate throw a wormhole near Saturn to another star before there is an oportunity. A great movie directed by Christopher Nolan.

2001: Space Odyssey

The classic master pice of the acclaimed director Stanley Kubrik has not lost its appeal for the science fiction public after many years from its presentation.
